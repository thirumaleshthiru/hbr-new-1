FAQ

Common
Questions

Answers to frequent questions about our engagement models.

How do you handle data security?

We adhere to strict global data protection standards (GDPR, CCPA) and use enterprise-grade encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Non-disclosure agreements are signed before any engagement begins.

What is your Hire-Train-Deploy model?

We source candidates based on your specific requirements, train them in our intensive bootcamps on your tech stack, and then deploy them to your project. This reduces your onboarding time and training costs significantly.

Can you scale the team if our project grows?

Yes, scalability is a core part of our offering. We have a deep pool of vetted talent and can ramp up team sizes within 2-4 weeks depending on the role complexity.
