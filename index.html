FAQ

Frequently
Asked Questions

Seeking basic information? Our FAQ section is a ready reckoner with precise answers to the most probable queries regarding our analytics, staffing, and consulting services.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in a wide range of industries including Retail, Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing, Energy, and Pharmaceuticals. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific compliance and operational needs of each sector.

How does your "Hire, Train & Deploy" model work?

We source high-potential talent, train them specifically on the technologies your project requires (like SAP, Data Analytics, etc.), and then deploy them to your workforce. This eliminates your training costs and ensures day-one productivity.

Can you help migrate our legacy systems to the Cloud?

Absolutely. We specialize in SAP migration and general cloud infrastructure setup. We ensure a secure, data-loss-free transition from on-premise legacy systems to modern cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or SAP Cloud.

Do you offer post-implementation support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-implementation support and maintenance packages. We monitor system health, provide updates, and assist with any technical issues to ensure continuous uptime.

How long does a typical consulting project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A specific staffing requirement might take 2 weeks, while a full SAP implementation could take 6-12 months. We provide a detailed roadmap during our initial assessment phase.
